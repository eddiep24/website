Mining Rig

From March, 2021 to August, 2021, I camped at Micro Center Dallas and bought 30 series GPUs to create and expand the Phillips mining rig. At its peak, the mining rig ran 51 GPUs, 10 CPUs, and several Helium miners in my former bedroom.
